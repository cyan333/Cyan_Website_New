Why becoming An Engineer

I was determined to be a circuit designer when I was a kid and I have to admit that my father has a huge influence on me. It was him to show me around his lab and it was him to teach me how to solder when I was only 10 years old. This is also why I treasure every opportunity to learn about circuit and programming. It is fair to say that being an good woman engineer is not an easy way to go, but I am proud of having the privilege to explore this magic field and consider it as my passion.

Thank you, Mum and Dad.
